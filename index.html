What is

SEO?

SEO stands for "Search engine optimation." What SEO involves is improving your websites visibility within different browsers through a variation of methods when people search for things such as products you sell, services you provide or information on anything your website is related to. In layman's terms this means we improve your websites visibility in order for your website to gets more clicks and traffic.
